About Me

I received my PhD in Economics from Aarhus University in 2017. During 2016, I had the opportunity to work under the guidance of Peter Neary at Oxford University. My BA is from Universidad de Buenos Aires, Argentina, which is located in my home country.

I have been working at the Economics Department of the University of Alberta since 2017. In 2023, I have been promoted to Associate Professor (with tenure). My research interests include International Trade and Industrial Organization. I am also interested in programming and use Julia as my main programming language.

My research primarily focuses on the microeconomic aspects of international trade, with a special emphasis on the role of superstar firms.
